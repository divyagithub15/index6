What is Travel Bubble? Updated List of India Travel Bubble

Travel bubble is a temporary arrangements between two countries aimed at restarting commercial passenger services when regular international flights are suspended as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic. These are also known as travel bridges / corona corridors / bilateral bubble arrangement. These travel bubbles are reciprocal in nature, meaning airlines from both countries enjoy similar benefits.

Science says travelling improves your health

The minute you begin to plan a trip, your happiness level rises. The fact that you have a trip to look forward to makes you feel better about your health, well-being, family and quality of life. It was found out in a Research conducted by the professors of the University of Surrey that anticipating a travel experience brings more happiness than the anticipation of buying something new. So, go ahead and plan that trip!

UAE Exempts Visa Fee For Children Under The Age of 18

The tourism of UAE takes a new leap and welcomes the children of tourists under the age of 18 to enter the country without any visa fee. UAE has opened the gates from July 15th to September 15th, during which all the children of the tourists, who are under the age of 18 will be exempted from the visa fee. Things Every Traveller needs to know Before Going Abroad

Travelling abroad requires a passport without exception. If you are applying for a passport for the first time, be sure to give yourself plenty of time before planning the trip. It is recommended that you check with your country’s state department to see what countries require a visa. There might be also times when you lose your passport. To prevent such situations, it is necessary to keep a photocopy of your passport in a different bag.
